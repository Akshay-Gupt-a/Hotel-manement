The Prime Hotel

The Hotel Prime offers 80 elegant and plush rooms decorated and furnished in a classical style with warm colors, quality furnishings and rich fabrics. Situated close to noida sector 15 Metro Station. The hotel prime also offers free parking, a gorgeous outdoor sun terrace, a hotel lounge room serving an extensive complimentary breakfast to all of our guests.
